About Dither.Live

Dither.Live is a free, open-source, web-based image editor that runs entirely in your browser—no downloads, no plugins. Powered by WebAssembly and modern JavaScript, it delivers lightning-fast performance even on large images, so you can focus on creativity instead of installation.

Choose from a suite of industry-standard and experimental algorithms—Floyd-Steinberg, Atkinson, Jarvis-Judice-Ninke, Bayer Ordered, plus threshold, pixelation, gamma and blur controls—all adjustable in real time. Every operation happens locally, ensuring your images stay private and your workflow stays seamless.

Source Code

Built with WebAssembly and modular JavaScript, Dither.Live is a fully open-source web-based image editor designed for clarity, extensibility, and efficient browser-based image processing. Developers interested in image manipulation, interactive web apps, or performance optimization are encouraged to contribute by submitting pull requests to our GitHub repository.

Your privacy matters.

Dither.Live processes all image editing entirely within your browser meaning no images are ever uploaded to our servers, and we never collect personal data or track your usage. Enjoy peace of mind knowing your projects remain secure and completely private at all times.
